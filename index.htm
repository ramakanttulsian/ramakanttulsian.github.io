    Ramakant Tulsian

    1955-2021

    Ramakant Tulsian, born on September 19th, 1955 to Paramanandji & Narmadadevi Tulsian in Mumbai. Born to an advocate father and homemaker mother, he was the ninth among eleven siblings and loved by all of them. He was loved and cherished by his family and he was always there for them through thick and thin. He was a man of very few words but great deeds. He believed in helping everyone and making their wishes come true. He was a self-made man who helped everyone around him lead a better life.

    There are no goodbyes for us. Wherever you are, you will always be in our hearts.

    Ramakant Tulsian

    - Born September 19th, 1955

    - Brother, Husband, Father, Grandfather.

    - Sadly left us on April 10th, 2021.
